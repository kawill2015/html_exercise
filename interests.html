What Kate Does with Her Down Time

Kate is a plant lover like her parents and enjoys spending any extra time outside working with plants.

This summer Kate started growing a pineapple plant in her parents' greenhouse and hopes it survives long enough to produce fruit.

Kate is addicted to pineapple and buys three to six pineapples at a time when they are on sale at the grocery store.

Kate also loves baking and cooking and has a really cool cookie cutter collection. Her new favorite set of cutters is a kit that includes Tupac, Notorious B.I.G., and Eazy-E's faces. They are so cool! Kate is a big fan of rap music!

Kate enjoys exercising and spends a lot of time riding her Brompton bicycle. Cruising through the Boulder Creek path is one of her favorite things to do.

Kate also enjoys playing ping-pong.
